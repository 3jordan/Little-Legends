About Little Legends Program

Latch-Key is a before and after school program that provides an atmosphere of fun and learning. Each child has the opportunity to receive assistance with homework, socialize with other children, express their creativity, play a variety of board games, use the computers, and participate in small and large motor activities. All of Latch-Key staff is CPR/First Aid certified.

LATCH-KEY DELAYS & CLOSINGS

When school needs to be closed or shortened because of inclement weather, the Latch-Key program will also be cancelled for the day. In the event of a delay, the morning session will still operate on normal hours. If Latch-Key closes after a delay, parents or a person on the emergency list will be contacted to pick up the child. It is important that parents make arrangements for children in the event of an unexpected school closing. Please be sure your phone number is up to date in the office.

Schedule

Latch-Key operates daily, Monday through Friday, except where noted due to scheduled school closings.

  • Morning Session: 6:30-8:30 a.m.
  • Afternoon Session: 3:30-5:30 p.m.

Fees

There is a non-refundable $15.00 annual registration fee per child. State paid childcare is accepted.

Morning OR Afternoon Session: $25.00 per week for the first child and $20.00 for each additional child.

Morning AND Afternoon Session: $45.00 per week for the first child and $35.00 for each additional child.

Late Fee Charges after 5:30pm*

  • $10.00 will be added to the account for 1-15 minutes past the end of the program
  • $20.00 will be added to the account for 15-30 minutes past the end of the program

*State paid childcare will not cover late fees.

FAQs

What are your operating hours?

Our operating hours are Monday through Friday from 6:30 AM to 8:30 AM for the Morning Session and from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM for the Afternoon Session.

What is the late fee policy?

If a child is picked up 1-15 minutes past the end of the program, a $10.00 late fee will be added to the account. If it's 15-30 minutes past the end of the program, a $20.00 late fee will be added.
